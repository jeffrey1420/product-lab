## Signal 3: n8n — No-Code AI Agents, Self-Service Threat

**Source:** n8n.io AI Agents page (April 2026)

n8n is positioning itself as the "world's most popular workflow automation platform" with AI agents. Key features:
- **Human-in-the-loop guardrails** — deterministic logic + AI
- **Self-hostable** — Business plan for <100 employees, Enterprise for compliance/governance
- **SOC2 compliant**
- **180k+ GitHub stars**
- **14-day free trial**, no credit card required
- **500+ integrations** including any LLM or vector store
- **MCP Server support** — can call n8n workflows from other AI systems

**Competitive threat:** n8n is the self-service alternative to Alizé. A French PME with a technical team could spin up n8n, connect Mistral AI, and have their own AI workflow automation without Alizé.

**Why Alizé still wins:**
- n8n requires technical setup and ongoing maintenance
- Governance and containment still need expertise
- French regulatory compliance (EU AI Act, GDPR) still need human judgment
- n8n is a tool; Alizé is an outcome

**Alizé counter-positioning:** "n8n is powerful, but you still need someone who knows what they're doing. We are that someone — and we operate the workflow, not just build it."

**Competitive note:** n8n's SOC2 compliance and enterprise governance features validate that compliance/governance is a real market requirement — exactly what Alizé leads with.
