## Signal 1: UniClaw — Managed OpenClaw Platform Validates the Model

UniClaw (uniclaw.ai) launched as a managed platform for OpenClaw AI agents, from $12/month. This is the most direct market validation for Alizé's concept:

- **What it is:** Fully-managed OpenClaw agent — skills, tools, messaging, app publishing, AI model credits
- **Pricing:** From $12/mo, with add-on AI credits
- **Target:** Anyone wanting an AI agent without self-hosting
- **Key differentiator vs Alizé:** UniClaw is a generic managed agent platform. Alizé is a French SMB specialist that builds domain-specific agents (accounting, CRM, invoicing) pre-configured for French business tools

**For Alizé:** UniClaw proves the "managed AI agent service" pricing model works. Alizé's edge is vertical specialization — not "here's an agent, configure it yourself" but "here's an agent that already knows French accounting, French invoicing law, and your Sage installation." The positioning is: "UniClaw for the 10-person PME, pre-loaded with what they actually need."

---

## Signal 2: Kestra — French Workflow Orchestration With Enterprise Clients

Kestra (kestra.io) is a French open-source orchestration platform used by JPMorgan, Toyota, and Crédit Agricole. Relevant signals:

- **What it does:** Declarative workflow orchestration for data, AI, and infrastructure — 1200+ plugins, event-driven, self-hosted or cloud
- **Key quote:** "Agentic Automation, Built In" — their platform includes native AI agent orchestration
- **Target:** Large enterprises with complex cross-team workflows
- **Alizé angle:** Kestra validates the orchestration space but targets engineering teams at large companies. Alizé targets the 10-person French PME that can't self-host Kestra or hire an engineer to maintain it. Different market segment entirely.

**For Alizé:** Kestra's enterprise success validates the workflow orchestration market. The gap Alizé fills: "I run a 15-person accounting firm in Caen. Kestra is not for me. But I still need my invoices followed up automatically and my client reminders sent."
