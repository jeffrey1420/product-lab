# RUNBOOK — MSP Prospecting (Managed AI Service)

## The Service
Selling managed AI agents as a service to SMBs. You find businesses, pitch them, install OpenClaw on your server, run their AI agents. They pay a monthly retainer.

## Target Client Profile
**Primary: Agencies and digital-native SMBs** — these get software subscriptions, understand SaaS, and have clear communication/workflows to automate.

**Best fit sectors (in priority order):**
1. Marketing / communication agencies
2. Web / IT agencies
3. Consulting firms and strategy consultants
4. Insurance brokers
5. Real estate agencies
6. Accounting firms (expertise-comptable)
7. Architecture / design studios
8. Event planning agencies
9. Recruitment / staffing agencies
10. Professional services (lawyers, notaires)

**Secondary fit (acceptable but harder sell):**
- Clinics and healthcare practices (reception, appointment scheduling)
- Real estate property managers
- E-commerce businesses with high inquiry volume

## HARD EXCLUSIONS — Do not prospect these
- Artisans / tradespeople (plumbers, electricians, landscapers, HVAC) — they think in physical jobs, not software, won't pay monthly
- Restaurants and bars — too much churn, low tech maturity
- Retail shops (boutiques, shops) — unless they have significant online presence
- Solopreneurs (1 person businesses) — no organizational buy-in
- Enterprise / big companies — too complex a sale

## Targeting Criteria
- 5-25 employees in Caen / Caen la Mer (Caen, Hérouville-Saint-Clair, Ifs, Lisieux, Ouistreham, Bayeux, Mondeville, etc.)
- Clear digital footprint: website with services, possibly a CRM or scheduling tool
- Business model based on client communication (inbound leads, quotes, appointments,follow-ups)
- Decision maker has email publicly visible (gérant, directeur, associé)
- Signs they need automation: high inquiry volume, online booking, multiple communication channels

## Value Proposition (reframe per sector)
Not "AI agents" — concrete outcomes:
- Agencies: "Handle client briefs 24/7", "Qualify inbound leads before you call back"
- Consultants: "Never miss a meeting request", "Auto-schedule and send prep notes"
- Brokers: "Answer policy questions instantly", "Capture every lead after hours"
- Clinics: "AI receptionist that books appointments", "Handle cancellations and rescheduling"

## Pricing Tier to Reference
- Starter: €299-499/month (1 agent, 1 workflow, 50 messages/month)
- Pro: €799-1,499/month (2-3 agents, 3 workflows, 200 messages/month)
- Enterprise: custom

## Tone
Professional, direct. French emails. Lead with their problem, one concrete benefit, one CTA. Keep emails under 100 words. No fluff.
