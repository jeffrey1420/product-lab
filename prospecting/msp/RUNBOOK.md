# RUNBOOK — MSP Prospecting (Managed AI Service)

## The Service
Selling managed AI agents as a service to SMBs. You find businesses, pitch them, install OpenClaw on your server, run their AI agents. They pay a monthly retainer.

## Target Client Profile
- Businesses with 5-25 employees in Caen / Caen la Mer
- Pain points: repetitive tasks, no automation, missing leads, slow customer response
- Sectors: service businesses, agencies, professional services, e-commerce, healthcare practices

## Targeting Criteria
- Businesses with clear operational bottlenecks
- Companies already using some digital tools (email, CRM, scheduling)
- Decision makers reachable by email or phone
- Exclusions: too small (solopreneurs), too large (enterprise), out of zone, competitors

## Value Proposition
Not "AI agents" — concrete outcomes:
- "Handle customer inquiries 24/7"
- "Your sales team never misses a lead again"
- "Automate the 3 hours of weekly admin tasks"

## Pricing Tiers to Reference
- Starter: €499-999/month (1-2 agents, 1 workflow)
- Pro: €1,999-4,999/month (3-5 agents, 3-5 workflows)
- Enterprise: €5,000+/month

## Tone
Professional but direct. In French for emails. Lead with their problem, not your solution.
