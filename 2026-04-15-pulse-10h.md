## Signal 3: US AI Giants Crack Down on Model Distillation (April 8)

A significant policy development: OpenAI, Anthropic, and Google jointly moved against model distillation — the practice of using outputs from frontier models to train competing models. Reported April 8, 2026.

**What this means:**
- Distillation has been how many open-source and smaller models close the gap with GPT-4/Claude
- If distillation becomes blocked or legally contested, it slows down model commoditization
- European models (Mistral, AMI Labs if it launches) face a harder path to closing the capability gap
- **Implication for Alize:** Alize's value proposition becomes stronger if model commoditization slows — the orchestration and governance layer matters more when the underlying models are differentiated
