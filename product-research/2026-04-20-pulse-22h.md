## Signal 1: Mistral Launches Voxtral TTS — French Voice AI Is Now Real

Mistral released **Voxtral TTS** (text-to-speech) in April 2026 — their first TTS model and a direct challenge to ElevenLabs.

**Key details:**
- 4B parameters — lightweight, low latency, cost-effective at scale
- 9 languages including French (France dialect explicitly supported)
- Outperforms ElevenLabs Flash v2.5 on naturalness in zero-shot custom voice context
- Parity with ElevenLabs v3 on emotion-steering — can do happy, sarcastic, neutral, etc.
- Voice adaptation captures personality, natural pauses, rhythm, intonation — not just reading text
- Available in Mistral Studio/AI Studio

**Why this matters for Alizé:**
This is the missing piece for French-language voice agents. Until now, building a French AI phone agent or voice assistant meant using ElevenLabs (expensive, US-centric) or accepting robotic French TTS. Voxtral changes that — native-quality French voice AI at Mistral's pricing, with full European data sovereignty.

Concrete use cases now viable for French PME:
- AI phone receptionist for salons, clinics, small retailers (appointment booking, basic Q&A)
- Voice agent for businesses without website — phone-first automation
- French-language outbound call agent (following up invoices, appointments)
- Voice-enabled CRM assistant that calls prospects and logs outcomes

**Strategic note:** Mistral is systematically building a full French AI stack — models (Mistral, Codestral), agents (Agents API), and now voice (Voxtral). Alizé building on Mistral's platform = French AI stack alignment that sells itself as "hosted in France, built for French business."
