## Signal 1: GetVocal AI — Competitive Brief (Deep Dive)

*This is the most direct French competitor to Alizé identified at VivaTech 2026. Full competitive brief filed at: `/data/workspace/product-research/competitors/getvocal-ai-brief.md`*

**What GetVocal does:**
- Hybrid human-AI agents for enterprise customer experience (CX/customer support)
- NOT a chatbot — a managed agent workforce that handles customer conversations autonomously
- "Context Graph" architecture: combines LLM fluency with deterministic rule enforcement
- Agents know when to hand off to humans, when to request validation, when to escalate

**Funding:**
- €22M Series A (November 2025)
- Led by Creandum (early Spotify backer), with Elaia and Speedinvest
- Total raised: ~€25M
- Notable: European tier-1 investors, not just French

**Key architectural insight:**
> "Where others stall with LLM-only agents, GetVocal breaks through. Our unique approach combines the natural fluency of LLMs with the precision of a Context Graph."

This is exactly the same positioning Alizé should use for its "governance + orchestration" layer — deterministic guardrails + generative capability. GetVocal has executed this in CX; Alizé can apply the same model to back-office operations.

**Where GetVocal is vulnerable (Alizé angle):**
1. **Vertical focus:** GetVocal is CX-only. Alizé's advantage is breadth — finance ops, HR workflows, logistics admin, etc.
2. **Pricing:** Enterprise CX contracts are expensive and often require 6-12 month sales cycles. Alizé's sweet spot (50-250 employee PME) is below GetVocal's typical deal size.
3. **No sovereign AI angle:** GetVocal likely uses generic LLMs (GPT-4, Claude). Alizé can differentiate on Mistral AI compatibility + French data residency.
4. **No operational workflow focus:** GetVocal handles customer-facing conversations. Alizé handles back-office operational tasks. Different buyer, different urgency, different value chain.

**Threat level for Alizé: MODERATE** — Not direct competition today (different vertical/segment), but if GetVocal expands to back-office automation, they become a direct threat. Track at VivaTech.
