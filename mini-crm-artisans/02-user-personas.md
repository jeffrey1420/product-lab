# User Personas — Devis & Factures Tool

## Primary Persona: Marc (THE customer)

**Who:** Marc, 34 ans, plombier-chauffagiste (heating engineer). Solo, works from home. Wife does the books.

**Reality:**
- Uses WhatsApp for everything — client contacts, quotes, photos
- Quotes sent via WhatsApp (PDF or screenshot of Word doc)
- Invoices on paper or Word, sent by email or WhatsApp
- Loses client details constantly
- Has a note on his phone with "clients importants" that he loses
- No system, no process
- Charges €50-80/hour, busy from October to March
- Quiet in summer — cash flow pressure

**Quote:** "J'envoie mes devis par WhatsApp, mes factures par email, et je sais plus ce que j'ai envoyé à qui."

**Tech level:** Comfortable with WhatsApp, Facebook, banking apps. Will not learn a complex tool. If it takes more than 2 minutes to create a quote, he won't use it.

**Jobs per week:** 8-15
**Invoices per month:** 15-30
**Admin time:** 2-3 hours/week (too much)

**What he wants:** Create a quote in 2 minutes, send it, forget about it until the client pays.
